**CADRays** Welcome to CADRays site. It's the simple and easy to use renderer and CAD processor based on [OpenCASCADE](https://www.opencascade.com/). ![User interface of CADRays](screen.jpg) Overview =============================================================================== CADRays is fast GPU-accelerated, unbiased, physically correct renderer. It means that CADRays uses the graphics cards to render photo-realistic images really fast. And physical correctness allows to achieve stunning results faster, with minimum parameters to tweak. Efficiency ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- CADRays uses highly optimized GPU Path tracing kernel and acceleration structures from OpenCASCADE. Interactivity ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- CADRays' on-screen viewport is the final render. Any changes to the scene are instantly updated on the screen allowing you to tweak any setting and view the results immediately. Materials ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Simple but robust 2-layer material system allows for variety of possible surfaces from glass and ceramic to sophisticated car paint. CAD oriented ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- STEP, IGES, and BREP formats are supported out of the box as well as all modeling capabilities of OCCT with interactive TCL console. Mesh support ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Conventional triangulation formats such as OBJ, PLY, STL and such are supported.
